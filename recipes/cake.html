Cake

Description

White Vanilla cake with frosting.

  • Cake mix
  • Egg
  • Frosting
  • Water
  • Oil
  1. pour cake mix into bowl
  2. add water to viscous density
  3. add 2 eggs
  4. mix thouroughly
  5. line baking pan with oil
  6. bake at 360 degrees for 45 minutes
  7. remove from oven carefully
  8. add frosting to top and ends of cake after removing from pan
  9. Add candles, light them and serve
