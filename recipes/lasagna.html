Lasagna

Description

Its a lasgana - pasta layers and meat + cheese. Its good.

  • pasta layers
  • ground beef
  • cheese (any kind)
  • parsley (optional)
  1. cut pasta into layers
  2. layer them into baking pan
  3. cook ground beef lightly in a pan first
  4. layer the beef on top of the pasta layer
  5. cover beef in cheese
  6. layer pasta over the beef and cheese
  7. Repeat this for another layer
  8. Place dish in oven and cook at 350 deg Farenheit
  9. leave for 45 minutes
  10. Remove from oven and cut into "squares"
  11. add parsely on top and serve
