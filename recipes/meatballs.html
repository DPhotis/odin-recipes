Meatballs

Description

classic Italian meatballs - cheese and parsley on top with onion & garlic beef mix

  • ground beef
  • onion
  • garlic
  • oregano
  • parsley
  • parmesean
  1. chop onion & garlic
  2. mix onion/garlic with ground beef
  3. add spice
  4. roll into balls using hands & place in cooking pan
  5. cook in oven at 350degrees
  6. remove after 35 minutes and salt/season to preference
  7. add cheese and parsley
  8. serve
